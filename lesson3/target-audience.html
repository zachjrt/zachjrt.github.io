Target Audience

The name of This site is Arizona Weather News


Personas

Edmond Dantes - As a daily commuter from his hometown of Gilbert to Chandler Arizona, Edmond finds himself traveling the area consistantly. He works at Intel, where they encourage carpooling. Edmond drives two of his coworkers to work every morning. He checks his phone to see if there are any expected delays on his route. He is fluent in most forms of Technology

Mollie Wardaemon - She recently moved to Arizona with her two children. As a stay at home mother, she tries to find activities to help keep her children active. She moved from a very rural area out of state, and Arizona is a major change of scenery for her. She mostly uses her phone for Youtube and Email.


Scenarios

  • Edmond needs to see if the weather is clear in both Giblert and Chandler, can he do this?

    Yes. Although the areas are nearby, he can specify the location directly

  • Mollie is predominantly worried about the temperatures throughout the day, can she check that?

    She'll be able to. The forcast for the temperature can be found on the site

  • Edmond likes to try to give his coworkers a heads up if the weather is supposed to be bad. Can he check the predicted weather for the week?

    Because the Weather is forcasted, he can check and give his coworkers plenty of heads up.

  • Mollie is constantly on the move, will she be able to use her phone?

    Yes the website will be usable and useful from many devices.

  • Edmond wants to be able to share the weather information with his coworkers, can he do this?

    Yes the website will have an ability to share information
