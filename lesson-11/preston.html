Wind shift

A recent development on the weather front of Preston is the shift of wind patterns. Often during this time of the year we have northerly wind that comes intermittently throughout the day. But a large cold front has shifted it so that we have been getting a southern wind during most of the morning period. This is expected to hold out for the rest of the season. Temperatures should be largely the same, but the chances of precipitation have gone up significantly. So you may consider carrying an umbrella in your car this spring.
